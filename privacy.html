Last updated: June 25, 2025

1. Overview

EssentialMC ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use our website or download our mod.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the service:

  • Download counts (anonymous, no personal data attached)
  • Basic server-side access logs (IP address, browser type, page visited) Ś stored for up to 30 days
  • Account information if you register: username, email address, and hashed password

We do not collect in-game data, player statistics, or any data from your Minecraft server.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the website and mod download service
  • To authenticate you when you sign in to your account
  • To monitor and improve site performance and stability

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies for session management only. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect login functionality.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Access logs are deleted after 30 days. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services for hosting and content delivery. These services have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their practices.

7. Children's Privacy

EssentialMC is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the issue tracker linked on the mod page.
